# Marketing Strategy - Pasta & Pastries by Cha

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** November 2025
**Business:** Pasta & Pastries by Cha
**Location:** Tanauan City, Batangas, Philippines

---

## Executive Summary

Pasta & Pastries by Cha is a family-run business offering homemade Italian comfort food and freshly baked pastries in Tanauan City, Batangas. This marketing strategy outlines a comprehensive, cost-effective approach to increase brand awareness, drive online orders, and establish the business as the go-to destination for quality homemade food in the local community.

**Primary Goals:**
- Increase online orders by 150% within 6 months
- Grow social media following by 300% within 3 months
- Establish strong local brand recognition in Tanauan City and surrounding areas
- Build a loyal customer base with 40% repeat customer rate

---

## 1. Market Analysis

### Current Market Position
- **Strengths:**
  - Fresh, homemade quality products
  - Family recipes with authentic taste
  - Competitive pricing (₱130-₱799)
  - Functional e-commerce website with online ordering
  - Pickup and delivery options
  - Custom order capability

- **Opportunities:**
  - Growing demand for convenient, quality homemade food
  - Limited local competition for authentic Italian comfort food
  - Untapped social media presence
  - Party catering and bulk orders market
  - Corporate partnerships and events

- **Challenges:**
  - Limited brand awareness outside immediate area
  - Competition from established restaurants and bakeries
  - Need for consistent social media presence
  - Building trust with new customers online

### Target Market Size
- **Primary:** Tanauan City population ~193,936 (2020 census)
- **Secondary:** Nearby cities (Lipa, Batangas City, Santo Tomas) - ~1.2M combined
- **Digital Reach:** Batangas province - ~2.9M population

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Audience Personas

#### **Persona 1: "Busy Family Maria"**
- **Demographics:** Female, 30-45 years old, married with children
- **Income:** ₱30,000-₱60,000/month (household)
- **Behavior:** Works full-time, values convenience, seeks quality homemade food for family
- **Pain Points:** Limited time to cook, wants nutritious meals, tired of fast food
- **Motivation:** Provide healthy, delicious meals without spending hours cooking
- **Preferred Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp groups

#### **Persona 2: "Event Planner Patricia"**
- **Demographics:** Female/Male, 25-40 years old, plans events/parties
- **Income:** ₱40,000-₱80,000/month
- **Behavior:** Organizes birthdays, corporate events, family gatherings
- **Pain Points:** Finding reliable caterers, ensuring food quality, staying within budget
- **Motivation:** Impress guests with quality food, hassle-free catering experience
- **Preferred Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, Word-of-mouth referrals

#### **Persona 3: "Young Professional Juan"**
- **Demographics:** Male/Female, 22-35 years old, single or young couple
- **Income:** ₱25,000-₱50,000/month
- **Behavior:** Busy professional, enjoys dining out or ordering in
- **Pain Points:** Limited cooking skills, craves comfort food, seeks value for money
- **Motivation:** Convenient meals, treat themselves, social media-worthy food
- **Preferred Channels:** Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Food delivery apps

#### **Persona 4: "Senior Citizen Lola Rosa"**
- **Demographics:** Female/Male, 60+ years old, retired or semi-retired
- **Income:** ₱20,000-₱40,000/month (pension + family support)
- **Behavior:** Values traditional flavors, prefers phone orders, enjoys sharing food with family
- **Pain Points:** Difficult to cook for small portions, wants familiar tastes
- **Motivation:** Nostalgic comfort food, easy ordering process, reliable delivery
- **Preferred Channels:** Phone calls, Facebook, Word-of-mouth

## 5. Marketing Channels & Tactics

### A. Social Media Marketing (PRIMARY FOCUS)

#### Facebook Strategy
**Why:** Largest user base in Philippines, best for local businesses, excellent targeting options

**Tactics:**
1. **Business Page Optimization**
   - Complete all business information (hours, location, menu, contact)
   - Add "Order Now" button linking to website
   - Enable Messenger auto-responses for quick inquiries
   - Create branded cover photo and profile picture

2. **Content Calendar (5-7 posts per week)**
   - **Monday:** Weekly specials/promotions
   - **Tuesday:** Behind-the-scenes kitchen prep
   - **Wednesday:** Customer testimonials/reviews
   - **Thursday:** Product spotlight with mouthwatering photos
   - **Friday:** Weekend special offers
   - **Saturday:** Customer feature/user-generated content
   - **Sunday:** Weekly recap/upcoming menu

3. **Facebook Groups Strategy**
   - Join 10-15 local buy-and-sell groups
   - Share posts (following group rules) 2-3x per week
   - Engage authentically with members' posts
   - Target groups: Tanauan City, Batangas food groups, mommy groups

4. **Facebook Ads Campaign**
   - **Budget:** ₱3,000-₱5,000/month (₱100-₱167/day)
   - **Objective:** Reach, Traffic, Conversions
   - **Targeting:**
     - Location: 5km radius from Tanauan Park Place
     - Age: 25-55
     - Interests: Cooking, Food, Italian food, Home delivery
   - **Ad Types:** Carousel ads (showcase multiple products), Video ads (food prep), Collection ads (menu browsing)

#### Instagram Strategy
**Why:** Visual platform perfect for food photography, high engagement rates, younger demographic

**Tactics:**
1. **Profile Optimization**
   - Bio: Clear UVP + location + order link
   - Story Highlights: Menu, How to Order, Reviews, Behind the Scenes
   - Business account with contact buttons

2. **Content Strategy (Daily posts + Stories)**
   - **Feed Posts (5-7/week):**
     - High-quality product photos (natural lighting, styled shots)
     - Flat lays, close-ups, serving suggestions
     - Customer unboxing videos
     - Process videos (time-lapse of making lasagna)
   - **Stories (Daily):**
     - Daily fresh batch updates
     - "Sold out" alerts (creates urgency)
     - Polls and Q&A sessions
     - Order confirmations (with permission)
     - Delivery/pickup moments

3. **Reels Strategy (2-3/week)**
   - Recipe teasers (15-30 seconds)
   - Satisfying food cutting videos
   - Packaging/unboxing content
   - Customer reactions (with permission)
   - Trending audio with food twist

4. **Hashtag Strategy**
   - **Branded:** #PastaAndPastriesByCha #PastaByCha
   - **Local:** #TanauanFood #TanauanCity #BatangasFood #BatangasFoodie
   - **Category:** #HomemadeLasagna #FreshPastries #ItalianFood #ComfortFood #PinoyCooking
   - **Size Mix:** 3 large (500k+), 5 medium (50k-500k), 10 small (5k-50k), 2 niche (under 5k)

5. **Engagement Strategy**
   - Respond to all comments within 2 hours
   - Like and comment on local food accounts
   - Follow and engage with potential customers
   - Collaborate with local food influencers (micro-influencers 1k-10k followers)

#### TikTok Strategy
**Why:** Explosive growth, viral potential, reaches younger audience, high organic reach

**Tactics:**
1. **Content Types (3-5 videos/week)**
   - Food prep ASMR (satisfying sounds)
   - Quick recipe tutorials
   - "Day in the life" of home baker
   - Packaging orders (satisfying)
   - Customer reactions
   - Trending challenges with food twist

2. **Optimization**
   - Use trending sounds and music
   - Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
   - Include text overlays and captions
   - Post at optimal times (7-9am, 12-1pm, 6-9pm Philippine time)
   - Cross-post to Instagram Reels and Facebook

## 6. Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

#### 1. Product Showcase (40% of content)
- High-quality product photography
- Process videos and time-lapses
- Product details and ingredients
- Size comparisons and serving suggestions
- New product launches

#### 2. Behind-the-Scenes (20% of content)
- Kitchen prep and cooking process
- Family story and values
- Team introductions
- Daily operations
- Quality control and ingredient sourcing

#### 3. Customer-Centric (25% of content)
- Testimonials and reviews
- Customer photos and videos
- Success stories
- Customer appreciation posts
- Community engagement

#### 4. Educational/Entertaining (15% of content)
- Cooking tips and tricks
- Food storage and reheating instructions
- Italian food culture
- Fun facts and trivia
- Seasonal recipes and ideas

### Visual Brand Guidelines

**Photography Style:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Warm, inviting tones
- Clean, simple backgrounds
- Show food in context (table settings, hands holding products)
- Mix of overhead shots and 45-degree angles

**Color Palette:**
- Olive Green (primary): #6B8E23
- Medium Beige: #D4A574
- Light Beige: #F5E6D3
- Sage Green: #9CAF88
- Neutral: #333333

**Video Style:**
- Authentic, unpolished (shows homemade nature)
- Captions for accessibility
- Upbeat background music
- 15-30 seconds for social (longer for YouTube)

### Content Creation Workflow

**Weekly Schedule:**
- **Monday:** Plan content calendar for the week
- **Tuesday:** Batch photo shoot (capture all products)
- **Wednesday:** Edit photos and videos
- **Thursday:** Write captions and schedule posts
- **Friday:** Create stories and real-time content
- **Weekend:** Engage with audience, respond to comments/messages

**Tools:**
- **Photo Editing:** Canva (free), Snapseed, Lightroom Mobile
- **Video Editing:** CapCut, InShot (free), iMovie
- **Scheduling:** Meta Business Suite (Facebook + Instagram), Buffer/Later
- **Design:** Canva Pro (₱499/month - worth the investment)

## 12. Success Factors & Recommendations

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Post daily on at least 2 platforms
   - Maintain brand voice and quality
   - Show up even when results are slow

2. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - Better to post 3 great posts than 7 mediocre ones
   - Invest in good photography/videography
   - Prioritize authentic, genuine content

3. **Engage Genuinely**
   - Respond to every comment and message
   - Build real relationships with customers
   - Show appreciation and gratitude

4. **Track and Adapt**
   - Review metrics weekly
   - Double down on what works
   - Kill or pivot what doesn't

5. **Stay True to Brand**
   - Never compromise on quality for growth
   - Keep the family/homemade story central
   - Let personality and authenticity shine through

### Quick Wins (Start These Today!)

1. **Optimize Google Business Profile** - Takes 2 hours, free visibility
2. **Create Instagram Business Account** - Add "Order Now" button to bio
3. **Join 5 Local Facebook Groups** - Start engaging today
4. **Take 20 Great Product Photos** - Use natural light, simple background
5. **Ask 5 Happy Customers for Reviews** - Easy social proof
6. **Create Facebook Event for Next Special** - Free reach to followers
7. **Set Up WhatsApp Business** - Better customer communication
8. **Design Branded Template in Canva** - Consistent brand presence
9. **Write 10 Social Media Captions** - Always have content ready
10. **Start Email List** - Add signup to website and receipts

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Inconsistent Posting** - Set realistic schedule and stick to it
2. **Buying Followers** - Fake engagement hurts more than helps
3. **Ignoring Comments/Messages** - Kills engagement and trust
4. **Over-Promotional Content** - Follow 80/20 rule (80% value, 20% sales)
5. **Poor Quality Photos** - Invest time in learning basic food photography
6. **No Clear CTA** - Always tell people what to do next
7. **Copying Competitors** - Be authentic, find your unique angle
8. **Spreading Too Thin** - Master 2-3 channels before adding more
9. **No Tracking** - Can't improve what you don't measure
10. **Giving Up Too Soon** - Marketing takes 3-6 months to show real results
